CalorieStack

CalorieStack is a minimal calorie counting web app. We make it as easy as possible to log calories by doing away with complex features and focusing on the basics. You don't have to register an account. Just create a new stack, bookmark the URL, and start logging calories. Or take a look at our sample stack to see an example of a stack already in progress.
